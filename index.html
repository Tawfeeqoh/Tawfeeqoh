01
Whitepaper + Brand Strategy
Multi-Chain DeFi Protocol — Full Brand Build
+
The Problem
Complex tech, zero story. Engineers wrote the whitepaper, for engineers. The landing page explained what but not why. No voice, no narrative thread.
My Approach
Story architecture first. Two audiences — DeFi natives and newcomers — required a dual-layer narrative. Precise enough for experts, clear enough for everyone else.
The Outcome
Brand transformed. Whitepaper became the canonical document. Landing page copy drove all external comms. Brand voice doc used to brief all future contributors.
02
Front-End Development
Web3 SaaS Site with Live Wallet Integration
+
The Problem
No dev, no budget for a team. A Web3 SaaS founder needed wallet connection, on-chain eligibility checking, and a conversion-optimised landing page.
My Approach
Pure HTML/CSS/JS + ethers.js. Built from scratch: animated hero, live wallet connection via MetaMask, ENS resolution, custom eligibility checker. Zero frameworks. Sub-1s load time.
The Outcome
Live and converting. Works across MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet, WalletConnect. Founder uses it as the primary conversion asset for investor and community demos.
03
Thread Writing × 3
Contest-Winning Thread Series
+
The Brief
Explain the unexplainable. Three contests, three topics — each needing a thread that was technically accurate, beginner-accessible, and compelling enough to win a community vote.
My Framework
Hook → Pain → Insight → Proof → Resolution. Every thread opens counterintuitively, establishes pain, delivers insight progressively, backs it with narrative, closes with a clear takeaway.
The Results
3 wins. 0 runner-ups. Each thread placed first. The consistency isn't luck — it's a repeatable system applied to any complex Web3 topic.
04
End-to-End Copy
NFT Launch — Zero to Full Brand Voice
+
The Problem
Brand existed in the founder's head only. Strong art direction, no written identity. No lore, no copy direction, no consistent tone. Launch was 3 weeks out.
My Approach
Brand sprint. Wk 1: voice discovery, lore framework, naming conventions. Wk 2: mint page copy, announcements, Discord copy, FAQ. Wk 3: launch copy, Twitter campaign assets.
The Outcome
Launch executed on time. Community responded to the lore consistency. Brand voice doc used to brief all future community managers. Consistent tone maintained post-launch.
